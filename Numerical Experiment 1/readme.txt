Dataset in this example is in file "database_faces_256x256.zip".
This dataset is the Chicago face dataset, which consists of male and female faces of varying ethnicity.
Specifically, we consider a subset of 10 images.
Reference:
D. S. Ma, J. Kantner, and B. Wittenbrink, “Chicago face database: Multiracial expansion,” Behavior Research Methods, vol. 53, pp. 1289–1300, 2021
